Bug Reporting and General Comments

The original MARS web page is maintained at http://www.cs.missouristate.edu/MARS/ and will contain updated releases, bug lists, etc.

The MM MARS repository is maintained at https://github.com/PrOF-kk/MM_MARS_Assembler and contains MM MARS-specific updates and bug reports

For bug reporting and general comments about the original MARS, please send an email to Dr. Pete Sanderson at PSanderson@otterbein.edu with "MARS" in the subject line. Please include sufficient information to recreate the problem, and assembly programs if appropriate.

For bug reporting and discussions about MM MARS, please open an issue at https://github.com/PrOF-kk/MM_MARS_Assembler/issues
